  • WHOIs

    Find Domain Identity & Ownership

    • WHOIS is the platform (record listing) through which one can identify the owner of the domain and the contact details of the owner of the domains. The Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN) regulates the domain ownership and domain name registration. All you need is the website address to know and search for the contact details of the domain owner.


    Every year after year, millions and millions of government institutions, organizations, businesses and individuals also register their domains names online. Each registered domain consists of data such as name, address, phone numbers, email address, administrative and technical data which gets stored in WHOIS data. WHOIS has firmed its root since the year 1982. As the Internet grew, WHOIS began to serve the needs of the stakeholders such as property and trademark owners, businesses and individual owners.
